IT Work

Alexander Lim

Alexander Lim is a Business Solutions Architect at Willis Towers Watson. Alexander’s profession primarily includes providing technology solutions to resolve business issues. His job encompasses both the technological and business aspects, requiring him to be very well versed in these topics to be able to provide precise solutions. Although the profession up front may seem to just provide solutions and ideas for issues, this profession does require the technical skills and expertise such as database management, modeling, and programming in different languages.

His profession requires working and interacting with a wide range of teams and people, however the main people he interacts with are the department managers as they are the ones responsible for determining certain types of technological requirements. The profession will also require meeting with different technological departments, including the platforms team, networking team, security team, and database team. It is overall a vast range of departments that the profession will require working collaboratively.

The profession primarily spends its time working on solutions design, managing the projects, and doing development. Also known as a BAU (Business As Usual), his work describes this as providing solutions for the business and troubleshooting issues, which is also another bulk of his work.

From the interview alone, it seems that he discusses that the most challenging aspect of his work is providing the solutions for the team. This is essentially the main role of his profession, designing solutions that fulfill the business requirements.
